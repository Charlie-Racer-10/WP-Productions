ORIGINS

The company was founded on the 16th October 2023 by two people: Charles Nutalaya Williams and Charly Moore-Parsons. The company was created as a way to compete against their rival "ZJEFFAD Studios", and even today the two companies are rivals. When the company was created, Charles Williams and Charly Moore-Parsons shared the company, but now, after Charly stepped down as co-owner, Charles has to manage WP Productions all by himself while Charly formed his own business called "SMERL" where he would create his own projects.

While WP Productions is relatively a new company, we still thrive to produce the best quality products while minimising budgets.
